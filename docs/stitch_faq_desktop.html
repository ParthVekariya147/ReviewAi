Frequently Asked Questions

How does ReviewAI collect reviews? expand_more
ReviewAI seamlessly integrates with major platforms like Google Maps, Yelp, and Trustpilot. We use authorized APIs to fetch your latest reviews in real-time, centralizing them in your dashboard for easy management and automated response generation.
Can I customize the automated AI responses? expand_more
Absolutely. While our AI drafts high-quality, contextual responses out of the box, you can define your brand voice, set specific templates, and always require manual approval before any response is published publicly.
What happens if I exceed my monthly review limit? expand_more
If you reach your plan's limit, we will continue to ingest new reviews so you don't lose data. However, automated AI responses will be paused until the next billing cycle. You will be notified in advance and have the option to upgrade your plan seamlessly.
Is my customer data secure? expand_more
Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. We are SOC 2 compliant and strictly adhere to GDPR regulations to ensure your and your customers' data remains private and secure.
